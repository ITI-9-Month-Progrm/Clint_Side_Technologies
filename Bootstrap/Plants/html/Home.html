Plants

are a major group of living things, such as trees, flowers, herbs, weeds, shrubs, and others. The plants are divided into: seed plants, avascular plants, ferns, and fern-like. Plants represent about 8.7 million species around the earth, which are divided by land and sea, and represent about 99% of their Bio-mass .

Medicinal plants are considered as one of the most important sources of medicines. More than 80,000 species are being used as medicinal. over 80% of the world’s population depends directly on it and about 40% of the pharmacological industries mostly rely only on medicinal plants.

The primary purpose of growing ornamental plants is to enjoy their beauty. It help to purify the air in homes and make it fresh. It has a psychological benefit, as it reduces depression and increases happiness rates

Aquatic plants are live in aquatic environments such as ponds, lakes, rivers, and seas. There are many types of aquatic plants. The depth of water is an important factor for these plants, because the greater the depth, the less light that reaches them, and these plants do not resemble much of land plants.

The types of plants found on planet Earth differ; Some of them are fruitful, some are for decoration, some are what we see on the surface of the earth, and some are found in the seas, and the number of plants on the surface of the earth is estimated at more than eight million species.
